Example of Search Tool

Yahoo! Search is a search tool created by David Filo and Jerry Yang (WordStream, n.d).
It original started as a collection of webpages that included man-made description with
each indexed URLs and considered to be one of the pioneers of
the Internet (WordStream, n.d). It is still be used by many people these days.

Google Search is perhaps the most commonly used Search Tool on the World-Wide-Web.
According to some research, it handles more than three billion search requests
per day (Burns, 2008), and hold a 64% market share in the US (ComScore, 2016).
